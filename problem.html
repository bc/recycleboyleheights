If you don't recycle, that's a problem! Here's why:
  1. Trash can hurt animals who live in the ocean or on land.
  2. Unrecycled trash can cause air pollution.
  3. If put in the wrong bin, items that could have been recycled will make landfills bigger.
  4. Sea animals might think floating, unrecycled trash are food.
  5. Animals in sea have been found with trash inside their guts.

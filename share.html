How to share your passion for recyling on Instagram:

Share your recycling through Instagram

  • First, take a picture of yourself putting something recycleable into the correct bin
  • Then, create an Instagram post, and put #newboyleheights and #helptheworld
  • Also, write what the recycleable object is!
  • Finally share the photo to friends, family, or anyone you want!
